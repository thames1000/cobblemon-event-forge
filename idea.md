Here are recommendations your **Event Forge software could generate/create**, not just event ideas:

## Event Types to Generate

* **Bingo Event Generator** — creates randomized bingo boards with catch, battle, biome, shiny, and item objectives.
* **Team Hunt Generator** — splits players into teams and creates shared objectives.
* **Legendary Clue Chain** — generates multi-step clues that reveal a legendary spawn condition.
* **Safari Zone Event** — generates temporary themed spawn pools, rules, tickets, and rewards.
* **Outbreak Weekend** — generates boosted spawns for a selected type, biome, or Pokémon family.
* **Battle Factory Night** — generates battle rules, rental teams, bans, and prize pools.
* **Resource World Treasure Hunt** — generates clue text, loot rewards, and hidden chest/item objectives.
* **Fishing Tournament** — generates scoring rules, rare fish/Pokémon targets, and reward tiers.
* **Gym Challenge Week** — generates themed gym rules, leader teams, badges, and rewards.
* **Community Milestone Event** — creates server-wide goals that unlock better rewards.

## Fun Objective Generators

* **Catch X of a type**
* **Catch X in a specific biome**
* **Catch with a specific Poké Ball**
* **Catch during specific weather**
* **Catch only at night/day**
* **Catch a Pokémon with a certain nature**
* **Catch a Pokémon above/below a level**
* **Catch a shiny matching the event theme**
* **Defeat X wild Pokémon**
* **Win X trainer/Battle Tower battles**
* **Breed X eggs**
* **Find/craft/turn in specific items**
* **Visit specific coordinates or landmarks**
* **Complete objectives with a friend nearby**
* **Use only Pokémon caught during the event**

## Reward Systems to Generate

* **Reward tiers** — bronze, silver, gold, champion.
* **Participation rewards** — everyone gets something small.
* **Winner rewards** — top player/team gets special rewards.
* **Community unlocks** — better rewards unlock as the server progresses.
* **Mystery crates** — randomized reward tables.
* **Risk/reward choices** — claim now or continue for better rewards.
* **Event tokens** — earn tokens, spend in temporary shops.
* **Cosmetic titles** — “Storm Chaser,” “Safari Master,” “Bug King.”
* **One-time legendary spawn tickets**
* **Battle Factory passes**
* **Safari tickets**
* **Shiny hunt boosters**

## Features That Would Make the Software Better

* **Random Event Button** — generates a full event instantly.
* **Theme Picker** — Electric, Fossil, Ghost, Ocean, Dragon, Baby Pokémon, etc.
* **Difficulty Slider** — casual, normal, hard, chaos.
* **Reward Balance Warning** — tells you if rewards are too strong.
* **Datapack Validator** — checks folder structure, JSON, pack format, namespaces.
* **Discord Announcement Generator** — creates a ready-to-post event message.
* **Rules Board Generator** — creates sign/rules text for players.
* **Admin Checklist Generator** — tells you where to upload files and what commands to run.
* **Leaderboard Template** — generates a score sheet or JSON tracker.
* **Event Archive** — keeps old events so you can rerun them later.
* **Event Calendar** — plan upcoming weekends.

## Most Entertaining Additions

* **Bingo boards**
* **Team competitions**
* **Server-wide legendary unlocks**
* **Mystery objectives**
* **Rotating Safari Zones**
* **Reward crates**
* **Battle events using only Pokémon caught during the event**
* **Chaos modifiers every 10–15 minutes**
* **Clue-based legendary hunts**
* **Community milestone events**

My top feature recommendation: add a **“Generate Full Weekend Event”** button that creates the datapack, objectives, reward crates, Discord post, rules board, and admin checklist all at once.
